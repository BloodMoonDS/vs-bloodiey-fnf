Girlfriend

Girlfriend, alternatively referred to as the Girlfriend, is the deuteragonist of Friday Night Funkin'. She is Boyfriend's partner and the daughter of Daddy Dearest and Mommy Mearest. She is frequently seen sitting on top of speakers, bopping her head to the tempo of the music, cheering Boyfriend on and keeping track of combos. While not considered an antagonist, she is the first opponent faced by Boyfriend, teaching the player how to play the game with her only track, Tutorial.
